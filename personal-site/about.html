Chess

I enjoy the critical thinking aspect and strategizing element that is needed to be successful at the game. Thinking 3-5 moves ahead is a difficult challenge, but results in benefits that can positively affect your way of thinking in day to day life aside from just the game. Not only is playing chess just an entertaining hobby I enjoy, but it has helped me to be more open to different solutions that I would never consider when solving a problem.

Basketball

I love to play basketball and watch it especially. Just like chess, I really enjoy the strategizing part of basketball and the thinking behind offensive and defensive schemes. You do need physical skills and intangibles to be a great basketball player, but without the "being a step ahead" mindset then it will definitely be hard to win games. Exercising your mind and body while having fun is something that is very important to me and that causes me to be drawn to the joy of basketball.

Gaming

When I want to relax and hang out with some friends, I like to play video games to be able to just push aside my worries for some time and enjoy a virtual world. I enjoy playing open world games, such as Legend of Zelda and Pokemon, but I do play competitive games to hang out with some friends and compete with each other. Gaming has always been apart of my life since I was 5 due to seeing family members always playing on a Playstation. That drew my interest into the hobby and I have not let go of it since then.
